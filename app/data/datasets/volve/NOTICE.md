# Volve dataset — placeholder

The Volve open dataset is a future addition to the Bulk Load registry.
Nothing is vendored in this folder yet — all three tiers in
`dataset.json` are disabled with `reason="not yet vendored — see
backlog"`.

## Upstream

Volve is published by Equinor under the OSDU open-test-data umbrella:

- **Source:** <https://community.opengroup.org/osdu/data/open-test-data>
- **License:** Apache License, Version 2.0
- **Original publisher:** Equinor ASA (released to the Volve community
  for research and educational use).

When Volve is vendored in a future release, the loader manifests and
any required schemas will land alongside this NOTICE.md and the
descriptor's tiers will flip to `enabled: true`. Until then this folder
exists only to (a) prove the registry handles ≥2 datasets and (b)
document the bring-your-own-dataset shape by example.
